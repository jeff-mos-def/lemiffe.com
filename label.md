**Lemiffe Records** is an indie record label and studio based in Belgium.

The label serves as a host of experimental and fusion genres, anything that combines or experiments with rock, folk, electronica, jazz fusion, electro-house, synthwave/outrun, electro-funk, ambient, experimental and chillout.

We currently only focus on the marketing and promotion of Lemiffe's own music but may in the future open up to other artists. Whenever this happens this page will be updated to include a roster, news, events and other information.
